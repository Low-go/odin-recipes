Brigadeiro



Delicious Brazilian desert


Ingredients


  • Butter
  • Cocoa powder or a prefered choclate of your choice
  • Condensed milk

Steps

  1. Throw butter, condensed milk and powder in a pan
  2. after mixing for a while turn on the fire and heat up pan
  3. mix continuosly without stopping until the mix is somewhat of a sludge
  4. after allow it to cool down, in teh firidge and then put butter on your hands
  5. Make them into balls and then put sprinkles on it
